How it works

It really comes down to three things.

You don't have to do it all in one sitting. Pick one, get it done, come back for the next. Paratus keeps track of where you left off.

STEP 01

Get the alert

Warnings for your actual address, so you're not finding out from a neighbor's text after it's already coming down.

STEP 02

Build the kit

Water, power, medications, the papers you'd hate to lose. We'll tell you what's worth having for what actually happens where you live, not a garage full of gear you'll never touch.

STEP 03

Make the plan

Where you'd go, who you'd call, what you'd grab on the way out. Much easier to sort out now, while things are calm, than in the middle of it.
