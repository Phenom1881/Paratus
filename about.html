About

We're the people who show up after.

Paratus comes from emergency managers who spend their days on disaster recovery. We built the thing we kept wishing every household already had.

Why we built it

It usually comes down to the same thing.

Disaster recovery is the day job. That mostly means showing up after the worst has already happened, walking neighborhoods, and sitting across from people on the hardest day of their year.

And you start to notice a pattern. The families who came through okay weren't lucky, and they weren't rich. They'd just done a few simple things ahead of time. A little water put back. A plan for where to meet. A way to get a warning.

The ones who got hit hardest almost always said the same thing: I kept meaning to get to it. Not careless, just busy, like everyone. The official advice was a wall of checklists they never finished.

So we built Paratus to be the opposite of that wall. Plain language, only what matters for where you live, done a little at a time. The tool we wish we could hand to every person before the storm instead of after.

What we believe

A few things we won't budge on.

Free, and staying that way

Getting ready shouldn't depend on what you can pay. Paratus is free, with no premium tier holding the useful parts hostage.

Your data is yours

We don't sell your information or run ads against it. We collect as little as we can and we're honest about the services that keep the site running.

No fear, no hype

We're not here to scare you into anything. Just clear, practical steps from people who have seen what actually helps.

Built for real places

Including small towns and rural counties that get overlooked. It works the same whether you're in a city or forty minutes from one.

Get in touch

We're a small operation and we read everything. If something's broken, confusing, or missing, or if you'd like to put Paratus to work where you live, we'd genuinely like to hear from you.
